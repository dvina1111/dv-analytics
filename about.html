About us

DV Data Analytics is a leading data science training and consulting firm, committed to bridging the gap between industry requirements and candidate skills. Our aim is to build relationships based on honesty, transparency, and trust with both our students and corporate clients. We prioritize delivering practical, industry-oriented knowledge to our candidates.

Key Highlights

DV Data Analytics, as a Data Science Training Institute in Bangalore, has established itself over the last 12 years by offering innovative academic programs and maintaining strong industry connections.

  • Enrolled more than 2500 candidates
  • 95%+ success rate in placements
  • 17.5 lacs pa. as an average salary
  • Faculty strength from industry experts
  • Placements in top MNCs including Morgan Stanley, Amazon, Flipkart, etc.

Consulting Services

Our consulting team consists of senior industry professionals with expertise in Telecom, Banking, Insurance, E-commerce, and Retail sectors. We aim to optimize organizational growth through a data-driven approach, offering solutions in:

  • Data quality and management challenges
  • Business intelligence and MIS reporting and automation
  • Technology migration and process improvements
  • Advanced analytics solutions and strategy building
  • Developing automated actions using Machine Learning and AI
  • Model deployment and execution
